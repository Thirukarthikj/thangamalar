The Aranthangi Heritage

Founded in Historic Aranthangi

The Aranthangi Heritage

Founded in the historic town of Aranthangi, Tamil Nadu, Thangamalar Jewellers began as a vision to honor the rich tradition of South Indian goldsmithing. What started as a modest atelier has blossomed into a sanctuary for those who seek uncompromising purity and artistic brilliance.

For generations, we have served the community not just as merchants, but as custodians of their most precious memories. Our artisans blend ancient techniques passed down through centuries with modern design sensibilities to create pieces that are both timeless and relevant.
